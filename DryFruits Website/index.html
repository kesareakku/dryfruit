Our Services

There's a wide array of delightful dry fruits to explore, each offering a unique and satisfying taste sensation. While numerous options abound, we're here to guide you through the world of dried fruits, helping you discover the perfect balance of flavor, nutrition, and quality.

Almonds(बादाम)

बादाम or Almonds are a nutrient-rich dry fruit known for their crunchy texture and mild, nutty flavor. They are a powerhouse of essential vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats. Almonds are a popular choice for snacking and are often used in desserts, smoothies, and almond milk.

Cashews(काजू)

काजू or Cashews are creamy and buttery nuts with a subtle sweetness. These kidney-shaped delights are a source of protein, healthy fats, and essential minerals. Cashews are not only a delicious snack but also used in Indian cuisine for curries, sweets, and more.

Pistachios(पिस्ता)

Pistachios, also known as पिस्ता, are green nuts with a slightly sweet and savory taste. They are rich in antioxidants, fiber, and nutrients, making them a heart-healthy choice. Pistachios are often enjoyed as a nutritious snack and are used in ice creams, pastries, and salads.

Raisins(किशमिश)

Raisins, also known as किशमिश, are sweet and dried grapes. These small, wrinkled fruits are packed with natural sugars and make a delightful snack. Raisins are a versatile ingredient used in baking, salads, and various culinary creations.

Walnuts(अखरोट)

अखरोट or Walnuts, are brain-shaped nuts with a distinct earthy flavor. They are a good source of omega-3 fatty acids, known for their brain-boosting benefits. Walnuts are a versatile ingredient in both sweet and savory dishes, from baked goods to salads.

Dried grapes(मुनक्का)

मुनक्का or Dried Grapes, are grapes that have been dried naturally, resulting in a chewy and sweet treat. They are a natural source of energy and are often consumed for their health benefits ,are a common inclusion in trail mixes and traditional remedies.
